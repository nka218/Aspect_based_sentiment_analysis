Overview

Sentiment analysis is contextual mining of text which identifies and extracts subjective information in source material, and helping a business to understand the social sentiment of their brand, product or service while monitoring conversations and reviews. We are using aspect based sentiment analysis. Aspect-based sentiment analysis is a text analysis technique that breaks down text into aspects (attributes or components of a product or service), and then allocates each one a sentiment level (positive, negative or neutral).

Objective

This tool allows us to identify sentiments of the customer from their reviews given on the hotel portal. Along with the customers sentiment about the service and offerings given by the hotel.
